FAQ

Common questions

What if I'm not in tech? Can I still get interview practice?

Yes - behavioral interview coaching translates to any field. Structuring your answers, telling a compelling career story, handling tough questions under pressure - none of that is exclusive to tech. If you want help with the communication and storytelling side of interviews, I can work with you regardless of your industry. Where my coaching is most specific to tech is in system design, live coding, and technical role prep.

What roles are you most experienced coaching for?

I have the deepest experience with data engineering, analytics, backend engineering, and DevOps - but I've also interviewed and coached candidates for product management, technical program management, and engineering leadership. If you're unsure whether your role is a fit, just reach out.

How is this different from practicing on my own or using a prep site?

Prep sites give you problems. I give you feedback. The biggest thing candidates are missing is an actual interviewer telling them what landed, what didn't, and why. You can solve LeetCode problems all week and still fail because of how you communicate. That's what I focus on.

Do I need to book multiple sessions?

No. Every session stands on its own - book what you need, when you need it. That said, many people come back for a second round after implementing their feedback, which tends to be the most productive session of all.
